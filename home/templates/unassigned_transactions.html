🤖 Auto-assign using rules

This will:

  • Set all unassigned incomes to TD CHEQUINGS.
  • Set some unassigned expenses to TD CHEQUINGS or TD AEROPLAN VISA based on category and vendor (e.g. Groceries at Costco → Chequings, other Groceries → Visa).
  • Skip categories that are handled by the transfer reclassification step (Arnprior Property Tax, RRSP Contributions, Foxview Down Payment Savings, Arnprior Rental Tax Withholding (LOFT/MAIN)).
  • Never change any transaction that already has a bank account.

🔄 Reclassify certain expenses as transfers & fix others

This will:

  • Arnprior Property Tax: for amounts ≤ $550.00, convert to a Transfer from TD CHEQUINGS to Wealthsimple Cash. Amounts > $550 are left as expenses for manual review.
  • RRSP Contributions: convert to a Transfer from TD CHEQUINGS to Wealthsimple RRSP.
  • Foxview Insurance payments of exactly $250.00: convert to a Transfer from TD CHEQUINGS to Wealthsimple Cash.
  • Foxview Down Payment Savings and Arnprior Rental Tax Withholding (LOFT/MAIN): always convert to a Transfer from TD CHEQUINGS to Wealthsimple Cash (no threshold).
  • For the above categories, the original expense rows are deleted after transfers are created.
  • Also fixes some categories that remain as expenses:
    • Arnprior Snow Removal, Foxview Property Tax, Subaru Insurance, Arnprior Heat, Foxview Internet → expense on TD CHEQUINGS.
    • Cell Phone, Arnprior Internet, Foxview Heat, Digital Subscriptions, Restaurants → expense on TD AEROPLAN VISA.
  • Only affects expenses with no bank account set. Existing assignments are not changed.

🪣 Attach buckets to reclassified transfers

This looks at Transfer rows whose description starts with Reclassified from expense: and can auto-link some of them to withholding buckets.

  • Reclassified from expense: Arnprior Property Tax → bucket Arnprior Property Tax.
  • Reclassified from expense: Arnprior Rental Tax Withholding (MAIN) or (LOFT) → bucket Arnprior Rental Income Tax.
  • Reclassified from expense: Foxview Insurance → bucket Foxview Insurance.
  • Other descriptions are left without a bucket for now.

🏠 Foxview Insurance cleanup

This helper cleans up historical Foxview Insurance entries so that the bucket matches both the real cash movements and the legacy ledger:

  • Finds Foxview Insurance expenses on TD CHEQUINGS with amounts of $250.00 or $500.00 and converts them into Transfer rows from TD CHEQUINGS to Wealthsimple Cash, tagged to the Foxview Insurance withholding bucket.
  • For the Foxview Insurance bucket, looks at legacy ledger entries with negative amounts (payouts). For each one that does not already have a matching expense, creates a Foxview Insurance Expense on TD CHEQUINGS, funded from that bucket.
  • Use this once after you've imported historical data, to bring the derived view in line with the legacy ledger.
